About Me

Hi there, I'm Maxwell! I'm a healthcare professional and creative arts therapist based out of Washington, DC. I've spent the past 6 years working with underserved populations, including folks with developmental disabilities and severe mental illness, through therapeutic uses of music. Now, I'm applying my skills of creativity, problem-solving, empathy, and curiosity to the world of data analytics, where I hope to continue working in healthcare.

This is my data analytics portfolio, where I'll share projects that I have worked on, including guided projects, challenges, and original projects.
